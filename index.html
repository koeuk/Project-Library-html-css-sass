Borrow and return books in just a few simple steps

How To Borrow Books

Choose a book and check whether it's available for borrowing. Bring it to the librarian, provide the necessary information, and complete any required forms to finish your request.

02

How To Return Books

Note the due date on your borrowing slip. Return the book within the two to three week period to the librarian to avoid any late fees.
